UCI REGISTRAR — ENROLLMENT POLICIES (EXCERPT)
Academic Year 2024–2025

ADD/DROP DEADLINES
──────────────────
Students may add courses during the first two weeks of instruction without
instructor permission. During Week 3, a departmental approval code (add code)
from the instructor is required. No adds are permitted after the end of Week 3.

Students may drop courses without a "W" (withdrawal) grade through the end of
Week 2. Drops during Weeks 3–10 result in a "W" on the transcript and do not
affect GPA. After the 10th week, drops require Dean's approval and are granted
only in cases of documented medical or emergency circumstances.

PASS/NO PASS (P/NP) POLICY
────────────────────────────
Undergraduate students may take a maximum of 16 units on a Pass/No Pass basis
per year, and no more than one course per quarter on a P/NP basis for courses
required by their major. Courses must be changed to P/NP by the end of Week 3.
A passing grade corresponds to a C or better.

GRADE FORGIVENESS (REPEAT POLICY)
───────────────────────────────────
UCI students may repeat a course in which they received a D or F grade. Upon
successful completion of the repeat, the original grade is excluded from the
GPA calculation, though it remains on the transcript. Each course may be
repeated once under this policy. Students may not repeat a course in which
they received a C or higher grade to improve their GPA.

ACADEMIC PROBATION
───────────────────
A student is placed on academic probation when their cumulative GPA falls
below 2.0. Students on probation must achieve a 2.0 or higher in the
subsequent quarter to be removed from probation. Failure to do so for two
consecutive quarters may result in dismissal from the university.

GRADE APPEALS
─────────────
Students who believe a final grade was assigned incorrectly must first discuss
the matter with the instructor within three weeks of the grade being posted.
If unresolved, the student may submit a formal grade appeal to the department
chair. Appeals must be filed within 30 days of the final grade posting.
